Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026 · Effective: April 19, 2026

The short version: CentryAI reads your email to find subscription receipts. We never store your emails, never sell your data, and never connect to your bank. Your data stays on your device by default.

1. Information We Collect

Email Access

To detect your subscriptions, CentryAI requests read-only access to your Gmail or iCloud inbox. We use this access solely to identify subscription confirmation and billing emails. We do not read, store, or transmit the body content of your emails. Only structured metadata (sender, subject, date, amount patterns) is processed locally on your device.

Account Information

When you create a CentryAI account, we collect your email address and a hashed password. We do not collect your name, phone number, or payment details unless you upgrade to CentryAI Premium.

Usage Data

We collect anonymized, aggregated usage data to improve the app — such as which features are used most often and general performance metrics. This data cannot be used to identify you.

Device Information

We collect your device type, operating system version, and app version to provide support and ensure compatibility.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Detect and display your recurring subscriptions
  • Identify zombie subscriptions based on usage patterns
  • Send renewal reminders (if enabled)
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve app performance and features
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties under any circumstances.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your subscription data is stored locally on your device by default. If you enable iCloud or Google Drive sync, your data is encrypted before transmission using AES-256. We do not have access to your encryption keys.

Our servers use industry-standard security practices including TLS 1.3 in transit, encrypted storage at rest, and regular third-party security audits.

4. Third-Party Services

CentryAI uses a limited number of third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In / Gmail API — for inbox scanning (governed by Google's privacy policy)
  • Apple Sign-In / iCloud — for inbox scanning (governed by Apple's privacy policy)
  • Stripe — for Pro subscription billing (we never see your full card number)
  • Sentry — for anonymized crash reporting

We do not share your personal data with any other third parties.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of anonymized analytics

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@centryai.app. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

CentryAI is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via in-app notification or email at least 14 days before changes take effect. Continued use of the app after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
